Title: Firefox can be dragged and dropped either on right or left side of the screen

2. Drag and drop Firefox 1 to the left side of the screen.        
3. Drag and drop Firefox 2 to the right side of the screen.        
Expected Result: 1. ...
2. Firefox is put on the left side of the screen.
3. Firefox is put on the right side of the screen.
